# Join Us

We're a fastly growing community of ambitious students who love learning. We encourage anyone and everyone to join!

## Who Can Join?
- Auburn University Students of any major or skill level

## How to Get Involved
1. Join our Discord
2. Come to a meeting
3. Sign up through AUInvolve
4. Meet, network, and build a node!

## Community Events
- For those outside of AU, we will have regular community-focused events to promote radio and mesh networking in Auburn and Opelika
- All are welcome!
